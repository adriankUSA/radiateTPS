About RadiateTPS

RadiateTPS is a cloud-based treatment planning system designed for researchers and medical professionals in proton therapy and radiation oncology.

Using OpenTPS as the backbone, this platform enables dose computation, CT scan management, and region-of-interest (ROI) processing, all through an intuitive web interface.
